Coaching Staff: Sid | Dave | Simon | Dan| Andrew | James | Chris | Yuhei | Employment

The British Football Academy Coaching Staff are certainly flying the flag for British football education here in Japan. As a unit they are a committed team, united in bringing and sharing the very best coaching methodologies and practices to young footballers from all over the globe who are living in Japan. Individually each and every one of them has a true 'front-and-center' passion for sharing their knowledge of football with the younger generations and collectively they form a unique group of superb British sports educators with the background, experience and qualifications that any football academy in the world would be envious of. The British Football Academy are extremely proud of the hard work, motivation, inspiration and impassioned approach our coaches bring to each and every session and we very much hope you get a chance to meet and have a chat with them, as they'd be delighted to meet and share a few moments with you.
