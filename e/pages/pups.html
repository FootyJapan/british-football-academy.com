Pups | British Bulldogs | British Lions | The Academy | Academy Stars | Under 15's

The British Football Academy "Pups" classes are the latest addition to the Academy family. It's never too early for children's enjoyment of football to be fostered and the beaming smiles on the faces of the 3 and 4 year olds attending these sessions are a testament to this.

Structured exercises incorporating balls with rhythmic exercises and fun games, serve to develop a very basic understanding of the game's fundamentals, whilst simultaneously promoting health, flexibility and bipedal development.

The sessions are kept to 30-minutes each, with a low coach:pupil ratio, to maximize the children's attention spans and ensure that the young enthusiasts' focus is held by our dedicated, lively, friendly and patient coaches/instructors week in and out, in an active, safe and progressive English-speaking environment.
