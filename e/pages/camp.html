British Football Academy Summer Soccer Camps 2019

"Football Fun in the Sun."
What better way for your kids to spend their time this summer?

All 6-12 year olds are welcome to join our age-specific summer soccer camps at 'TO BE CONFIRMED'. Let our English FA qualified, British coaches enthral, entertain and educate your young ones with their wealth of knowledge and lively, energetic characters. We encourage participants to enrol for the full 3 or 5 action-packed-days of football games and fun, but do accept players on a per-day basis*. All children will be presented with an official BFA Camp T-shirt and certificate of achievement.
REGISTER ON-LINE HERE
* Please note that we will prioritise campers who sign up for the full camp, and accept per-day basis campers where spaces are available. We will inform per-day basis campers of the availability 1 week before each camp starts.
