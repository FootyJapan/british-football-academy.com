British Football Academy Summer Soccer Camps 2014

"Football Fun in the Sun."
What better way for your kids to spend their time this summer?

All 4-14 year olds are welcome to join our age-specific summer soccer camps at British School Tokyo, Showa Campus in Tokyo and YC&AC in Yokohama. Let our English FA qualified, all-British coaches enthral, entertain and educate your young ones with their wealth of knowledge and lively, energetic characters. We encourage participants to enrol for the full 4 or 5 -action-packed-days of football games and fun, but do accept players on a per-day basis. All children will be presented with an official BFA Camp T-shirt and certificate.

Important information for participants:

  • Please have your child wear something comfortable to play football.
