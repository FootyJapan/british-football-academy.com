GROUND & FOOTWARE

The stunning all-weather surface is possibly the safest playing surface for children to practice and improve their football. Flat-soled football sneakers with good traction, or rubber 'bobble' soled futsal shoes are suitable for this surface (please see our Footware Chart) and we ask that all children wear shin pads when attending BFA sessions at Sarugaku Elementary School.

GETTING THERE

By Train:
7 mins walk from Daikanyama Station on the Toyoko line, or about 10 mins from Shibuya Station South Exit.

By Car:

There is paid parking next to the entrance to the school, but please do not park on the road as the school WILL complain to us.


RAIN PLAN:

In the event of rain, sessions can go ahead.
If there is heavy rain, snow (thick on the ground), lightning, or the ground is in poor condition due to earlier inclement weather the ground must not be used, and the sessions will be cancelled.
Please understand that there will be no refund or make up sessions in the event of cancelled sessions due to the weather/ground condition.
We will inform* you by 3:00 pm if the sessions will be cancelled via our Facebook page**: https://www.facebook.com/BritishFootballAcademy.

*Note: We will inform you only in case of doubtful/inclement weather. Please also understand that we might have to cancel after 3pm if the weather/ground condition worsens after 3 pm.
**you do not have to have a facebook account to veiw this page
