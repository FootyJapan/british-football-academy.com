Yuhei Yamaguchi - Academy Assistant Coach

  • British Football Academy - Assistant Coach
  • Arsenal Soccer School certification
  • Redbull Street Style Japan Final top 3
  • JHCA Physical conditioner
  • JAPAN Physical Organization Sports leader

Yuhei brings a unique perspective to BFA thanks to his history as one of Japan's top freestyle footballers. He has had a number of successes in this arena, most notably finishing third in the Red Bull all Japan Street Challenge in 2008. With such a distinguished string to his footballing bow it should come as no surprise to learn that Yuhei's playing style is one that utilises mesmerising close control, deft touches and mind boggling tricks. Skills that he endeavours to pass on to everyone he comes into contact with - children and fellow coaches alike!

As a coach Yuhei has a wealth of experience built up over a number of years working with children in Japan and Hawaii. His ever ready smile combined with his natural friendliness, dazzling skills and eagerness to impart his unique knowledge and skill set ensure that he is a highly popular and successful coach.

As part of his dedication to improving players skills he offers one on one private football training sessions. In these sessions - tailored to the individual needs of the student - Yuhei focuses on honing not just technical skills such as control, touch and feel for the ball but basic agility, co-ordination, balance and rhythm.
