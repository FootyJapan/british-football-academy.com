Tokyo Futsal Clube, Shibaura

Tokyo Futsal Clube, Shibaura is a newly built facility in a converted Warehouse. As it's a Warehouse it has the added benefit of having a roof that will keep it dry in the rain & shaded in the hot sunny summer months. One side is completely open though with just a net to stop balls going out, so it is well ventellated keeping things cool. Although the actual area of the court is smaller than our other locations, the surface is made of brand new, 3rd generation astroturf, which is great for playing on. The long bladed turf will protect children from injury should they fall over during practice and we reccomend rubber soled turf shoes for all players.

There are benches along the open side so that parents can sit down and have a good veiw of the children playing. There are also vending machines to buy drinks to make sure both parents & children are well hydrated. There are bathroom facilities including a shower on site.

GETTING THERE

By Car
There is limited parking available to the public at club, up to about 8 cars and there is also meter parking just down the street. It is approximately 10-minute drive from Shinagawa area and 15-minutes from Hiroo, Roppongi, Azabu-Juban etc. For those using car navigation, the following information may be useful: (address) 105-0023, Tokyo, Minato-ku, Shibaura 1-6-8. Tel: 03-6435-1611

By Train
Tokyo Futsal Clube, Shibaura is located midway between Tamachi & Hamamatsucho Stations on the JR Yamanote line (5~10 min walk), or very close to Hinode station on the Yurikamome monorail from Shimbashi.

By Taxi
Ask the driver to go to this address: Shibaura 1-6-8.
