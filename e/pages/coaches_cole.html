Cole Nagy - Academy Coach

  • Real Salt Lake Academy U16/U18 (2009-2012)
  • Canada U17 National team (winter/spring 2009-2010)
  • United States U17 National team (summer/fall 2010-2011)
  • NSCAA High School All American 2011
  • University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) Men's Soccer (2012-2014)
  • UCLA Summer Camps Coaching and Counsellor 2012-2014

My favourite footballer was always Paul Scholes because he is so well rounded and positionally smart. He wasn't afraid to put in the hard work and behaved, for the most part, with class and grace on the pitch. As a coach, I feel strongly about instilling a love and respect for the game into the kids by allowing them the freedom to explore their own aspects of football which they are most attracted to, while still guiding them to through the necessary fundamentals of the game... Keeping it fun but serious in a positive environment that builds confidence and skill simultaneously.
