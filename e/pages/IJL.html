International Junior League

• About: I`d like to inform you that Footy Japan will be starting a mini kids league ( International Junior League ) starting from January and running until March.



• Information: 1. For the inaugural league there will be 4 teams in both the U10 and U12 age groups.
2. Participating teams will be Jaguars, Nishimachi School, British School and FC Nomad.
3. Each team will play each other twice.
4. Games will be 35 minutes long.
5. Fixtures will be played at Sarugaku ( Tuesday 16:45~ ), Ikejiriohashi ( Saturday morning 9:00~ ), British School ( Saturday morning 9:30~ ).
6 Jaguars will be charged 1000 Yen for games at Ikejiriohashi or British School.

• Fixtures: Fixtures for U10 and U12 ( Subject to change )

Jan 13th V Nishimachi ( at Sarugaku )
Jan 24th V FC Nomad ( at British School )
Jan 27th V Nishimachi ( at Sarugaku )
Feb 7th V FC Nomad ( at Ikejiriohashi )
Feb 10th V British School ( at Sarugaku )
Feb 28th V British School ( at British School )

* U10 and U12 will play on the same day.

• Eligibility
We hope this league will be a success but a lot depends on the kids availability.
