BFA Member Card Discounts

BFA members will receive a 20% discount at the following 7 adidas performance centre/concept shops: Ginza, Roppongi Hills, Aqua City Odaiba, Shibuya, Shinjuku, Ikebukuro and Colette Mare, Minato Mirai.
The offer is for these shops only (not adidas RUNBASE) & BFA members MUST show their members cards to receive their discount.

Please note:
1) The latest store list is always updated on adidas Website. Some of the stores may change the merchandising or be added or closed next year.
2) Some products are not included for this discount (mi adidas/ball/sunglasses/watches etc). for details, please contact store staff when you visit the store. (this is same case with adidas Employees).
