Mitsui Housing Compound - ground / getting there / rain plan / fees / venue rules

Mitsui Housing Compound sits in the heart of Tokyo, a short distance from either Roppongi, Akasaka or Roppongi ichome stations. It is easily accessible from the Tokyo Midtown, Roppongi, and Akasaka areas.

GROUND & FOOTWARE

The classes are held on the Mitsui Compound Grass Area (or Gym in case of inclement weather or darkness).
All children are asked to wear flat-soled trainers (sneakers) or turf shoes for their own safety, football boots (cleats) with molded or screw-in studs will not be permitted on the grass (please see our Footware Chart).

GETTING THERE

Due to strict security measures at the housing compounds entry points, names of everyone attending classes must be given to the gate before the sessions, to permit entry for non EWA members.

By Car
Please do not go by car as you cannot take your vehicle into the housing compound.

By Train
The housing compound is located 5~10 mins walk from either Roppongi, Akasaka or Roppongi ichome stations

By Taxi
Ask the driver to go to this address: 2-1-1 Roppongi Minato-ku, Tokyo, 106-0032

RAIN PLAN:

In the event of rain, snow or lightning, the ground must not be used, therefore sessions will go ahead in the gym. Please make sure you change your shoes at the gym entrance. Parents/Guardians/Siblings are to change to indoor shoes as well.
We will NOT notify whether we will be in the gym or not on the day, therefore please make sure to bring both indoor (clean flat soled trainers) and outdoor shoes (flat soled trainers).
*Note: We will inform you in advance via email if there are any dates when the gym is not available. Please understand that there will be no refund or make up sessions in the event of cancelled sessions due to the gym being unavailable.
