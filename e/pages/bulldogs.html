Our "Bulldog" classes are for all children, regardless of gender or ability, between the ages of 5 to 6 years old.

The classes aim to provide a highly enjoyable and productive introduction to the game of football. Bulldog kids will relish the opportunity to play, learn and make new friends in a fun, pressure-free, international environment.

Through various games and activities Bulldog kids will develop basic ball skills, including passing, controlling and shooting. At the same time the development of self-confidence and the importance of team cooperation are actively encouraged and nurtured by BFA coaches.

All this is provided to the Bulldogs within a safe, fun, English speaking environment.
