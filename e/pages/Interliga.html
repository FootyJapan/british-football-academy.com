INTERLiga

• About: INTERLiga are matches played between the different BFA locations once a term at YC&AC on a Saturday morning. Children from both YC&AC and other loactions form teams to play matches against each other and some International school teams in a tournament format. This is held for the Bulldogs (U6), Lions (U8), Academy(U10) and the Academy Stars(U12) classes and its a chance for the children to gain experience of playing 'real' football matches in a tournament style setting.

Application: Please email: education.ops@footyjapan.com (deadline 1 week before each event date)
 
Participation fee: ¥3,000 to be added to the monthly fee of the following month
