Ohashi Junction, Ikejiri Ohashi/Shibuya

Ohashi Junction futsal court is a newly built facility inside the arena like Ohashi Junction (that connects the Shuto (246) Expressway with Yamate dori Expressway). The surface is made of brand new, 3rd generation astroturf, which is great for playing on. The long bladed turf will protect children from injury should they fall over during practice and we reccomend rubber soled turf shoes for all players.

There are benches on one side and a small park so that parents can sit down and have a good veiw of the children playing. There are also vending machines nearby to buy drinks to make sure both parents & children are well hydrated. There are bathroom and changing facilities on site.
* Parents: please do not wear heeled shoes, take prams/pushchairs or food onto the astroturf.

GETTING THERE

By Car
There is parking available to the public in a paid carpark inside the junction right next to the pitch, accessible from the small road next to the river.

By Train
Ohashi Junction is located close to the East exit of Ikejiri Ohashi Station on the Hanzomon/Shin-Tamagawa line (5 min walk), this is just 1 stop (2 mins) from Shibuya.

By Taxi
Ask the driver to go to this address: Meguro Ohashi Yubinkyoku (post office) it is then just along the small street next to the river!
