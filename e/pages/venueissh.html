International School of the Sacred Heart (ISSH), Hiroo - ground / getting there / rain plan / venue rules

International School of the Sacred Heart sits in the heart of Tokyo, just minutes on foot from Hiroo station. It is easily accessible from the illustrious Roppongi Hills, Motoazabu and Hiroo areas - all of which are popular residential areas of Tokyo's thriving expatriate community.

GROUND & FOOTWARE

The ground is a safety-approved, astroturf playground and as such all children are asked to wear flat-soled trainers (sneakers) or rubber soled 'futsal' shoes for their own safety. (please see our Footware Chart).

GETTING THERE

By Train
Take the Hibiya line to Hiroo Station and go out of Exit 4 (Nishi Azabu end). ISSH is just up the side road to your right as you come out of the exit.

By Car
Unfortunately parking is not permitted at the school although dropping off & picking up kids is fine. Do not drive right up to the school itself though & please be courteous to the guards at the school.

RAIN PLAN:

In the event of light rain, sessions will go ahead.

If there is heavy rain, snow or lightning, or the ground is in poor condition due to earlier inclement weather the ground must not be used. However, providing it is available,* sessions will go ahead in the ISSH Gym (enter from the main building entrance, walk along the corridor to the back of the school and go down the stairs to the gym on B2).
Please make sure you change your shoes at the school entrance. Parents/Guardians/Siblings are to change to indoor shoes as well.

We will NOT notify you whether we will be in the gym or not on the day, therefore please make sure to bring both indoor (clean flat soled trainers) and outdoor shoes (flat soled trainers for Sundays, trainers/rubber soled 'futsal' shoes for Saturdays).

*Note: We will inform you in advance via email if there are any dates when the gym is not available. Please understand that there will be no refund or make up sessions in the event of cancelled sessions due to the gym being unavailable.

VENUE RULES:

Please also make sure you read and comply the regulations in the BFA members guide.

At ISSH

  • Food and sugared drinks are not allowed on the playground. (water/sports drinks only)
  • No litter to be left anywhere. Please take your rubbish with you.
  • No Pets allowed.
  • No one is permitted access to ANY part of the facilities that BFA don’t use, including the hill area, the bushes & trees. We may use the play structures next to the ground but please make sure that all children are supervised by parents/guardians/nannies.
  • The toilets we are allowed to use are limited – Please make sure to keep them clean.
