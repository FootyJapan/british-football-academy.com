Refund Policy

  • The British Football Academy (BFA), will in no way and under no circumstances give refunds, unless the circumstances are deemed to be extenuating. For example if, in the unlikely event that a child is injured during a coaching session, to the extent that the child can no longer participate in future BFA. sessions and the injury was a direct result of negligence on the part of a BFA. official, then a refund will be made to cover the exact value of the outstanding classes purchased upon registration.
  • All care is taken by BFA to ensure that any BFA. apparel provided to a child upon registration, or purchased thereafter, is of the best possible quality and free from defects and/or flaws. In the unlikely event that said merchandise is indubitably flawed or defective and provided that said merchandise has not been worn, BFA. will exchange the merchandise contiguously.

Insurance Policy

  • The optional Accident Insurance is provided by SONPO JAPAN an independent insurance agent. Although insurance claims will be facilitated by BFA. BFA in no way and under no circumstances accept responsibility for rulings made by SONPO JAPAN following an insurance claim. In opting for the insurance plan it is understood that BFA. and its affiliates will accept no liability for accidents and injuries, including those resulting in death, provided that the accident or injury was in no way caused by the negligence of BFA personnel. All BFA. coaching staff have 3rd party general liability insurance cover. In the case where upon registering a child at BFA., said child does not have fully-comprehensive medical insurance, it is strongly recommended that said child gets coverage from BFA's optional/independent insurance plan provided by SONPO JAPAN.
  • Coverage: Accident insurance for kids. In the unlikely event that an injury or accident occurring during a coaching session results in death, coverage up to 9,540,000 yen is available within 180 days of the accident occurring.
  • Injury /post injury, from 3 to 100 % of 9,540,000 yen coverage, depending on the degree of injury and to be claimed within 180 days of the injury occurring.
  • Accidents and injuries resulting in hospitalization are covered at a rate of up to 5,000 yen/day (within 180 days)
  • Accidents and injuries resulting in the need for an operation(s) will be covered at a rate of x10, x20, x 40 of 5,000 yen depending on the medical care needed (within 180 days)
  • Coverage for doctor's fees are up to 2,500 yen/day, for a maximum of 90 days.
  • Any pre-existing health issues, including, but not limited to the following: asthma, diabetes, autism, epilepsy, attention deficit disorder, hyperactivity, depression, blindness, deafness, emphysema, that may affect a child's ability to participate in BFA's coaching sessions should be reported to BFA prior to participation in a coaching session. If a child has been ill or sick and the health condition of the child has warranted either a) an absence from school and/or b) visiting a doctor/hospital to receive medical advice, treatment or medication, then such matters must be reported to BFA office and BFA's coaching staff.
