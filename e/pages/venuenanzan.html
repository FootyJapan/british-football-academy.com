Nanzan Elementary School, Roppongi/Azabu Juban, Minato-ku. - ground / getting there / rain plan / venue rules

Nanzan Elementary School sits in the heart of Tokyo, just minutes on foot from the illustrious Roppongi Hills and is easily accessible from the Azabu Juban, Moto Azabu and Hiroo areas - all of which are popular residential areas of Tokyo's thriving expatriate community.

GROUND & FOOTWARE

The ground is a safety-approved, rubberized playground and as such all children are asked to wear flat-soled trainers (sneakers) for their own safety, football boots (cleats) with molded or screw-in studs will not be permitted on this surface (please see our Footware Chart).

GETTING THERE

By Train:
Take either the Oedo or Namboku subway line to Azabu Juban station. Walk up Azabu Juban high street almost all the way to Roppongi Hills. Just before the high street joins the major road (leading to Roppongi tunnel), there's a small road on the left that rises sharply around a corner. Nanzan Elementary School is located 100 meters along this road on the left-hand side, opposite Roppongi High School.

By Car:
Unfortunately parking is not available at the school itself, so parents bringing their children by car are asked to park in the nearby Roppongi Hills complex or one of the parking areas in Azabu Juban.

RAIN PLAN:

We will go ahead in the light rain either at Nanzan or at the International School of Sacred Heart (ISSH) in Hiroo (depends on the condition of the grounds at Nanzan), in case of doubtful weather we will inform you on the day by 12pm on our Facebook page*: https://www.facebook.com/BritishFootballAcademy.

*Please note: you do not have to have a facebook account to veiw this page

If there is heavy rain, snow (thick on the ground), lightning, or the ground is in poor condition due to earlier inclement weather the ground must not be used, and the sessions will be cancelled.
Please understand that there will be no refund or make up sessions in the event of cancelled sessions due to the weather/ground condition.

VENUE RULES:

Please also make sure you read and comply the regulations in the BFA members guide.

Way to/from Nanzan
Please be aware that cars, motorbikes and bicycles are NOT allowed to park on the street near the school entrance. The area is often patrolled by traffic wardens. (See below)
Also, please refrain from bringing/using skate boards & kick boards when commuting to/from Nanzan.
The main gates are locked at all times. Please use the door to enter - it will open from the inside.
Please make sure to close the door behind you.

At Nanzan

  • Food and sugared drinks are not allowed. (water/sports drinks only)
  • No litter to be left anywhere. Please take your rubbish with you.
  • No Pets allowed.
  • No one is permitted access to ANY part of the facilities that BFA don’t use = the play structures and inside school buildings are off limits. (See below)
  • Chin-up bar, stream & pond, jungle gym / monkey bars, sandbox and play tools, the rope hanging from the 2nd floor of the kindergarten building are all off limits.
  • The toilets we are allowed to use are limited – the access to the toilets are from outside (See below)
