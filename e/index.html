SESSIONS SCHEDULE UPDATE
You are all aware of the ongoing situation with the COVID-19 (Coronavirus) and how it is starting to affect daily life here in Tokyo. The Tokyo Metropolitan Government has now issued a general advisory to limit large gatherings in Tokyo "as much as possible" between February 22 and March 15. This is aimed more at larger events with many spectators and/or indoor events, so for the time being, we will continue to hold our classes unless the venue asks us not to.

Here are the locations & session staus at present (March 19th):

  • TAC - classes ON
  • EWA - classes OFF (until April 5th)
  • Sarugaku - classes OFF (until Mar 31st)
  • YC&AC - classes ON
  • Ohashi - classes ON
  • ISSH - classes OFF (until Mar 31st)
  • Nanzan - classes OFF (until Mar 31st)

We are continuously monitoring developments throughout the Tokyo area and will update you with any new information &/or class changes.
